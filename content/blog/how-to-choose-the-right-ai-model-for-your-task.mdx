# How to Choose the Right AI Model for Your Task

With dozens of AI models available, picking the right one feels overwhelming. Here's the truth: **the most expensive model isn't always the best choice**.

## Match the Model to the Task

### Quick Questions → Fast Models
Need a simple answer? Use GPT-4o-mini or Claude Haiku. They're fast, cheap, and surprisingly capable for routine queries.

### Complex Reasoning → Heavy Hitters
Multi-step logic, code architecture, or nuanced analysis? That's when you reach for GPT-5 or Claude Opus. The extra cost pays off in quality.

### Creative Writing → Claude
Claude models excel at natural, varied prose. If your content needs to sound human (not robotic), Claude is your best bet.

### Code Generation → GPT-5
GPT-5 leads in code completion and debugging. For technical tasks, it's the workhorse.

### Long Documents → Gemini
Need to process 100+ page documents? Gemini's massive context window (up to 1M tokens) makes it the clear winner.

## The Real Cost Calculation

Don't just look at per-token pricing. Consider:

1. **Time saved** — A better model might solve in 1 prompt what takes 5 prompts with a weaker one
2. **Rework avoided** — Cheap models that produce wrong answers cost more in the long run
3. **Context limits** — Exceeding context means starting over or losing coherence
